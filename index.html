Our Work as GotabGaa

Our Activities and Plans

Social Welfare

One of the primary functions of Gotab Gaa is to provide a social safety net for its members. This includes financial assistance during times of need, such as medical emergencies, funerals, or other unexpected expenses. By pooling resources and contributions from its members, the group ensures that no member is left without support during challenging times.

Investment Initiatives

Gotab Gaa recognizes the importance of economic empowerment in improving the quality of life for its members. Therefore, the group engages in various investment activities. These may include collective investments in businesses, agriculture, or real estate. By working together, members can access opportunities and resources that may be difficult to attain individually.

Read More

Community Development

Beyond the direct benefits to its members, Gotab Gaa also plays a role in community development. The group often initiates or supports projects and initiatives that benefit the broader community in Taita Taveta County. These projects may include infrastructure development, educational support, healthcare initiatives, or environmental conservation efforts.

Read More

Cultural Preservation

As a Kalenjin-based group, Gotab Gaa takes pride in preserving and promoting the cultural heritage of its members. This may involve organizing cultural events, celebrating traditional festivals, and passing down traditional knowledge to the younger generations, ensuring that the rich cultural identity of the Kalenjin people endures.

Social Cohesion

Gotab Gaa fosters a sense of belonging and unity among its members. Through regular meetings, gatherings, and collaborative activities, it strengthens social bonds within the group. This cohesion extends beyond individual and family units, contributing to a more closely-knit and supportive community
